    Early Life

    Ludacris was born in 1977 on 11th September as Christopher Brian Bridges. He was born to R0berta Shields as well as Wayne Brian Bridges. He was their only child. His birthplace was Champaign, Illinois.

    Later, he moved to Chicago, where he joined Emerson Middle School, Oak Park. Subsequently, he enrolled at Oak Park & River Forest High School. He stayed there for a year. For another year, Ludacris was a student at Centreville High School since he was residing in Centreville, Virginia. Then, he went to Banneker High School, Atlanta, Georgia, where he graduated in 1995. He was studying at Georgia State University between 1998 and 1999, pursuing music management.

    Ludacris and the late Richard Pryor, who was a comedian, were cousins.

    Career

    At the age of nine, Ludacris made his debut in music. That is when he began rapping. He became a DJ for a brief period and then released his debut album titled Incognegro (2000). That same year, he released another one called Back for the First Time. In 2001, 2003 and 2004, Ludacris released three more, namely Word of Mouf, Chicken-n-Beer as well as The Red Light District, respectively.

    In 2006 and 2008, Ludacris released Release Therapy and Theater of the Mind, respectively. In the 2010s, his albums include the Battle of the Sexes and Ludaversal.

    In addition to that, he is an actor best known for portraying the role of Tej Parker in 2 Fast 2 Furious. Other appearances are on New Year’s Eve, Gamer, Crash as well as Empire among others. He has also voiced characters in video games such as Def Jam series and Forza Horizon 2.
