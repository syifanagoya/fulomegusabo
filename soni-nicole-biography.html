    Who is Soni Nicole?

    Soni Nicole is an American actress and dancer. She has worked with some famous worldwide artists like Pitbull, Christina Aguilera, Justin Bieber, Missy Elliot, Kimbra, Sharaya J, and many more.

    Who are the parents of Soni Nicole?

    She was born Soni Nicole Bringas on 2nd February 2002 in Portland, Multnomah County, Oregon.

    Her father was born in Montevideo, Uruguay while her mother was born in Barcelona, Spain. She grew up along with her brother, Paul.

    She enrolled in dance class when she was only 5 years old. But her academic qualifications are not disclosed.

    Soni Nicole- Professional Life, Career

    Soni Nicole debuted her career in the TV show by playing the role of a hip-hop dancer in the CW’s romantic and satirical series, Jane the Virgin, in November 2014.

    In 2014, she performed as a dancer in the show, Dancing with the Stars.

    After that, she made her appearance in various TV series, including Beautiful and Twisted and Instant Mom, both performed in 2015.

    She rose to fame by playing the role of Ramona Gibbler on the Netflix series, Fuller House, from 2016 to 2019.

    Soni has also worked as a production manager for the short movie, An Act of Kindness, in 2019. In 2020, she is going to perform in the film, Path to Midnight.

    Moreover, she was credited with many commercials, including performances for brands like American Girl, the Jordache, and The Swagger Wagon Toyota.
