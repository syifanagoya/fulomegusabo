    Days of Our Lives spoilers and updates tease Marlena Evans (Deidre Hall) will confront Talia Hunter (Aketra Sevillian). It seems no one’s secret is safe in a small town like Salem, and Marlena accuses her of ruining her daughter Belle Brady’s (Martha Madison) marriage!

    The news that was supposed to have been kept secret has gotten out, that Talia and Shawn Brady (Brandon Beemer) got drunk and had grief sex and now Talia’s having his baby. T

    Shawn couldn’t turn his back on a baby he believed was his, and it took months and the revelation that the baby was Evan Frears’ (Brock Kelly) for Belle to forgive.

    Now, the betrayal and pain is fresh anew for Belle, fearing if she doesn’t lose him to liquor, she will to Talia!

    The timeframe is right-and the baby has to be Shawn’s, Talia’s tormentor was already in prison, even though Marlena thinks a paternity test needs done.

    Shawn has been on a downhill spiral ever since he accidentally put his father Bo Brady (Peter Reckell) in a coma, and recently blaming himself for Victor’s death as well.

    The drinking isn’t the problem, Shawn is, but Talia certainly hasn’t helped.

    Marlena, after dealing with a bereaved and angry Belle and Talia’s sister not using discretion, has had it up to there with Talia.
