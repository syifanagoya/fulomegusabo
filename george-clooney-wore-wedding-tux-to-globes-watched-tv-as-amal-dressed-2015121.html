    Just, swoon. George Clooney stole the show at the 2015 Golden Globes on Sunday, Jan. 11. The Monuments Men actor, who received the Cecil B. DeMille Award, was all about his new wife Amal Alamuddin.

    After gushing over the human rights lawyer during his acceptance speech, where he adorably said he "couldn't be more proud" to be her husband, the former bachelor told reporters that when it came to getting ready for the big night, he had it easy compared to his wife. 

     

    "I'm wearing my wedding tux," Clooney admitted, saying he was able to pull an outfit with a special meaning from his closet, while his new wife spent days comparing dresses. Clooney wore said tuxedo when he said "I do" to Alamuddin, 36, in Italy this past September.
