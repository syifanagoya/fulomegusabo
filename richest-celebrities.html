    What is Kevin Max's Net Worth?

    Kevin Max is an American singer, songwriter, and poet who has a net worth of $6 million. Kevin Max was born in Grand Rapids, Michigan in August 1967. He is best known for being a member of the Christian group DC Talk. Max served as the lead singer of the band Audio Adrenaline from 2012 to 2014.

    With DC Talk Max was featured on the albums DC Talk in 1989, Nu Thang in 1990, Free at Last in 1992, Jesus Freak in 1995, Supernatural in 1998, Solo in 2001, and Free at Last: The Movie in 2002. He has released the solo albums Stereotype Be in 2001, The Imposter in 2005, Holy Night in 2005, The Blood in 2007, Cotes d'Armor in 2010, Broken Temples in 2015, Starry Eyes Surprise in 2015, and Playing Games with the Shadow in 2016. Max released the album Kings & Queens with Audio Adrenaline in 2013. He has collaborated with other artists including Rich Mullins, Michael McDonald, Newsboys, Michael Sweet, and more.
