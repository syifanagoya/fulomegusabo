    What is Peter Engel's Net Worth?

    Peter Engel is an American television producer who has a net worth of $50 million. Peter Engle is best known for his teenage sitcoms that appeared on NBC on Saturday mornings, which was called TNBC. Engle is best known for his work on the television series Saved by the Bell which helped lead to his other shows including California Dreams, City Guys, and Hang Time.

    He worked with the Christian Broadcasting Network for nearly 20 years before joining Pat Robertson's Regent University. Engle has produced the TV series Sirota's Court from 1976 to 1977, Good Morning, Miss Bliss from 1987 to 1989, Saved by the Bell from 1989 to 1993, Saved by the Bell: The College Years from 1993 to 1994, California Dreams from 1992 to 1996, USA High from 1997 to 1998, Saved by the Bell: The New Class from 1993 to 2000, Malibu, CA from 1998 to 2000, Hang Time from 1995 to 2000, One World from 1998 to 2001, All About Us in 2001, City Guys from 1997 to 2001, and Last Comic Standing from 2004 to 2014. Engle has been nominated for a Daytime Emmy Award for Saved by the Bell: The New Class and for a Primetime Emmy Award for Last Comic Standing.
